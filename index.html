Why Jeevan Jhola?

Plastic pollution chokes our planet—26,000 tonnes of waste daily in India alone. Jeevan Jhola reimagines how we carry, with a community-powered system to replace single-use plastics with reusable solutions.

From bustling markets to global cities, we’re building a movement for a cleaner, greener future.

The Numbers Speak

  • 300M Tonnes Globally Yearly
  • 9% Recycled Worldwide
