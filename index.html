About Us

Jeevan Jhola is a sustainability-driven startup that aims to eliminate single-use plastic from daily life through community-powered circular systems.

Mission: Plastic-free, sustainable, youth-powered future.

Vision: A world where utility and responsibility go hand in hand.

"We’re not just reducing plastic—we’re building a movement." - Founder

How It Works

1

Take Bag

Pick up a reusable bag from our partner shops.

2

Use

Use it for shopping, deliveries, or daily needs.

3

Drop it off at any collection point.

4

Earn Points

Get rewards for every return.

Future Scope: Reusable containers, delivery systems, and awareness campaigns.
