Founder’s Vision

“I’m Akash Kumar—born in Bihar, studying AI & Data Science in Kerala. Plastic overwhelms our rivers and homes. Jeevan Jhola is my solution: a scalable, people-powered reuse system.”

India generates 26,000 tonnes of plastic waste daily—60% unrecycled. Globally, 300 million tonnes pile up yearly. We’re here to change that.
